Falling Fruit

View the Github Repo

Falling Fruit is a pygame application inspired by the Nintendo video game series Animal Crossing. It is written entirely in Python and imports the gamebox library put together by Luther Tychonievich. All images are taken from unofficial recreations of Animal Crossing themed spritesheets.

To play, click the left and right arrow keys to move Isabelle side to side in order to catch the various fruits before they hit the ground. There are six types of fruit (levels) and with each new fruit, the falling speed gets faster! Catch three of a kind in order to unlock the next level.
