Sound Sketches

Sound Sketches are a collection of p5.js sketches that use microphone input to create interactive visual displays. In these sketches, I play around with perlin noise and flow fields to create a sense of organic movement.

The cool thing about p5.js is that all the code renders beautifully within browsers and is compatible with html/css. This is somewhat of a personal investigation where I continue to experiment with new ways to incorporate sound with the potential of javascript libraries.

Click on the images above to play around with it in real time. Make sure to give your browser microphone access!
